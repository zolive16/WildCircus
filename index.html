About Us

"Time is a circus, always packing and leaving". By Ben Hecht

Compagnie Wild Circus was created in 2002 and is part of the contemporary circus world, drawing inspiration from different types of theatre (baroque, classical, street...). She uses the audacity and beauty of circus techniques. The company offers 5 creations that it takes to France and abroad.
